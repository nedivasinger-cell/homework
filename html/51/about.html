About Tuvya's Good Food

Established in aproximately 2017, Tuvya's Good Food is offers a unique dining experience. From pike schitzel caught by the might Tuvya to the herring and jam combo he insists the rest of the world only doesn't like because they never tried it yet, Tuvya's Good Food is not for the faint of heart. As well, Tuvya's Good Food strives to create an ambience like no other. With deer heads mounted on the walls, physics textbooks conviently placed on the tables, and Carlebach nigunim playing from the speakers, this is not a restraraunt that will ever be forgotten.
